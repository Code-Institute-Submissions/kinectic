Information

This is a word search game where you are given a set of wordList and you must find all of them to complete the game.

Once you choose a category and difficulty you will be taken to the puzzle, there you will have a timer that only stops when you finish the game.

Each difficulty will reward different points and the puzzle orientation also changes, the poinst system are as follows:

  • Easy Difficulty
    • Score is 10 points per found word.
    • Time Bonus is 50 points, if completed in 60 seconds.
    • Completion Bonus is 10 points.
  • Medium Difficulty
    • Score is 25 points per found word.
    • Time Bonus is 120 points, if completed in 120 seconds.
    • Completion Bonus is 20 points.
  • Hard Difficulty
    • Score is 50 points per found word.
    • Time Bonus is 200 points, if completed in 180 seconds.
    • Completion Bonus is 30 points.

Once a game is complete it will be saved and you will be able to see it under the menu on the right top corner in the Leaderboard. In there you will see the total time and score since you started playing linked to your username.
